# Guernsey Tax Submission Instructions

This README file contains instructions on how to use the Guernsey tax submission website.

## Overview

The Guernsey tax submission website allows users to submit their tax returns electronically. This guide provides step-by-step instructions to help you navigate the site and complete your submission successfully.

## Instructions

1. **Access the Website**: Open your web browser and go to [Guernsey Tax Submission Website](https://www.gov.gg/tax-submission).

2. **Register/Login**: If you are a new user, you will need to register for an account. If you already have an account, log in using your credentials.

3. **Navigate to Submission Section**: After logging in, find the "Tax Submission" section in the main menu.

4. **Fill in Your Tax Information**: Follow the prompts to enter your personal information, income details, and any deductions you may qualify for.

5. **Review Your Submission**: Once you have entered all information, review your submission for accuracy.

6. **Submit Your Tax Return**: Click the submit button to send your tax return.

7. **Confirmation**: After submission, you will receive a confirmation email. Keep this email for your records.

## Notes
